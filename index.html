EcoShelf

EcoShelf helps you track household items and expiry dates so you waste less food and money. Add items to virtual shelves, get notified before things expire, and keep your kitchen organised.

Features

  • Track items across multiple shelves and categories
  • Expiry-date reminders with customisable notifications
  • Offline-first — your data stays on your device
  • Clean Material You design with light and dark themes
